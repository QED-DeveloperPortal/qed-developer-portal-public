# Claude 101 for Beginners

Artificial Intelligence tools are everywhere today. One of the popular AI assistants is **Claude**.

If you've heard about Claude but aren't sure what it is or how it works, this guide will explain everything in simple terms.

## What is Claude?

**Claude** is an AI assistant that can read, write, explain, summarize, and help solve problems.

It is similar to tools like:

- ChatGPT
- Google Gemini
- Microsoft Copilot

Claude is designed to understand natural language — meaning you can talk to it like you would talk to a person.

Example:

> You: "Explain black holes in simple terms."  
> Claude: "A black hole is a place in space where gravity is so strong that even light cannot escape."

## Who Created Claude?

Claude was created by the company **Anthropic**.

Anthropic focuses on building **safe and helpful AI systems** that behave responsibly.

Claude can help with many everyday tasks.
