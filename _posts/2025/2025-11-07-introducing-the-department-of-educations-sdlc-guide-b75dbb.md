Have you ever wondered how software is designed, built and deployed within the **Queensland Department of Education**? If so, you are at the right place!

The GitHub repository [QED-DeveloperPortal/SDLC](https://github.com/QED-DeveloperPortal/SDLC/) is the ultimate guide to the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) created specifically for the **Department of Education, Queensland**. 

It serves as a **comprehensive documentation portal** — designed to help anyone who want to understand the **official process and standards** that govern software delivery within the department.  

## Why it matters  

This repository isn’t about *how to code* — it’s about *how to build software the right way* within the Department of Education.  

By following this SDLC guide, teams ensure that their applications are:  
- aligned with departmental policies  
- developed using consistent and approved practices  
- secure, maintainable, and fit for purpose  

Whether you are new in the department or looking to refresh your understanding, this documentation ensures your project meets the **required governance and compliance standards** and help you to understand what's required to **deliver quality solutions** across the Department of Education ecosystem.
